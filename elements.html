About Me

Education

A fresh graduate from Heriot Watt, I completed my Masters in Science in Global Sustainability Engineering, where I discovered the critical importance of sustainable processes and applications. My studies encompassed various subjects, including Green and Sustainable Logistics, Sustainable Processing, Process Engineering, Renewable Energy Technology, and Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA).

Thesis

My final year project, "Predicting Future Emissions of Scotland Using Data Science", was a compelling blend of Data Science and Sustainability. I applied machine learning techniques to forecast future emissions based on historical carbon emission data from the Scottish Government's website. This project successfully integrated my knowledge in both sustainability and data science.

Work Experience

My journey into data analytics began with my first role, where I gained proficiency in MySQL for data extraction and processing. I developed expertise in MySQL and subsequently utilized various dashboards for data visualization. My skills evolved to include data visualization with Excel and PowerBI. Transitioning my career further, I extensively adopted Python for advanced visualization, complemented by a certification from Udemy. During this period, I explored machine learning applications, leveraging Python libraries for stock data portfolio analysis.

Passion & Future Goals

I am deeply passionate about the power of data science and its influence to address critical challenges. My goal is to contribute to organizations that prioritize data-driven decision-making for a better more succesful future. I am continuously seeking opportunities to expand my skills in advanced analytics, machine learning, artificial intelligence and explore innovative applications.

Beyond the Professional
