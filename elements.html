About Me

Education

A fresh graduate from Heriot Watt, I completed my Masters in Science in Global Sustainability Engineering, where I discovered the horizons of sustainability and how critical it is to focus towards sustainable processes and applications. I studied various subjects, including Green and Sustainable Logistics, Sustainable Processing, Process Engineering, Renewable Energy Technology, Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA).

Thesis

My final year project was a mix between the application of Data Science in Sustainability processes and sustainable future. My thesis was titled as "Predicting Future Emissions of Scotland Using Data Science", in which I applied machine learning to predict future emissions based on the historical carbon emission data available on Scottish Government's website. This allowed me to successfully implement my current knowledge of both, sustainability and data science.

Work Experience

I worked to become an expert in MySQL, where further I utilised various dashboards for data visualizations. Later on, I employed data visualizations with Excel and PowerBI. Later on, with the shift in my career, I started using Python excessively for visualization, where I completed a certification from Udemy to learn the basics and applied it in my professional. In that specific time, I utilized machine learning and its applications where I utilized various Python libraries to extract stock data portfolio for stock analysis.
